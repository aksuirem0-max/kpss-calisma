Read the text and answer the questions
20 pts · 2x10
📖 Metin
My name is Helen. I live in a small apartment in a quiet neighbourhood. There are two bedrooms, a living room, a kitchen and a bathroom in our house. My favourite room is the living room because I usually watch TV and spend time with my family there.

My mother enjoys cooking in the kitchen, and my father waters the flowers on the balcony every evening. I share my room with my sister. There is a bunk bed, a desk and a small bookshelf in our room. After school, we usually do our homework and clean our room together.
Soru A
Why does Helen like the living room?
Cevap
She likes the living room because she usually watches TV and spends time with her family there.
Alternatif: "Because she watches TV and is with her family there."
Soru B
What does Helen's father do every evening?
Cevap
He waters the flowers on the balcony every evening.
Alternatif: "Her father waters the flowers on the balcony."

Read the text and answer the questions (Tigers)
20 pts · 4x5
📖 Metin
Tigers are one of the endangered animals in the world. They usually live in forests and grasslands in Asia. Tigers are strong hunters, but their population is decreasing because people destroy forests and hunt them illegally. Today, many organizations are working to protect tigers and their natural habitats. People should protect nature to help endangered animals survive.
Soru 1
What kind of animals are tigers?
Cevap
Tigers are endangered animals. / They are strong hunters.
Soru 2
Where do tigers usually live?
Cevap
They usually live in forests and grasslands in Asia.
Soru 3
Why are tigers endangered?
Cevap
Because people destroy forests and hunt them illegally.
Alternatif: "Their population is decreasing because of deforestation and illegal hunting."
Soru 4
What should people do to help endangered animals?
Cevap
People should protect nature to help endangered animals survive.

Complete with Simple Present or Simple Future Tense – Life in Space Hotels
20 pts · 10x2
📖 Metin (Boşluklu)
Nowadays, many people (1) [watch] science-fiction films about space travel. Scientists also (2) [develop] new technologies every year. In the future, space tourism (3) [become] more common. People (4) [stay] in space hotels and (5) [travel] with special spacecraft.

Experts think robots (6) [help] visitors in these hotels. Some people (7) [not believe] space holidays will be safe, but others (8) [want] to experience life in space. I think space tourism (9) [change] the travel industry in the future, and many people (10) [dream] about visiting space one day.
# Mastar Bağlam ipucu Doğru Form Zaman
1 watch Nowadays / many people watch Simple Present
2 develop every year / Scientists develop Simple Present
3 become In the future will become Simple Future
4 stay In the future / People will stay Simple Future
5 travel In the future / People will travel Simple Future
6 help Experts think … robots will help Simple Future
7 not believe Some people (genel kanı) don't believe Simple Present
8 want others (genel durum) want Simple Present
9 change I think … in the future will change Simple Future
10 dream many people (genel durum) dream Simple Present
Not – 10. boşluk: "will dream" de kabul edilir; "one day" gelecek anlamı taşıyabilir.

Speaking – Sorular ve Örnek Cevaplar
100 pts
a. What is your favourite traditional dish?
My favourite traditional dish is mantı. It is a traditional Turkish food.
b. Where do people usually eat this dish?
People usually eat mantı at home or in traditional restaurants.
c. What ingredients are used in your favourite food?
It is made with flour, minced meat, onion and garlic. People serve it with yoghurt and butter.
d. Why is this dish special for you or your culture?
It is special for me because my grandmother makes it on special days and it brings the family together.
e. Can you talk about an endangered animal?
I want to talk about tigers. They are one of the most endangered animals in the world.
f. Why is this animal in danger today?
Tigers are in danger because people destroy their natural habitats and hunt them illegally.
g. What should people do to protect endangered animals?
People should stop illegal hunting, protect forests and support animal protection organizations.
